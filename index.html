Travelers Rest

Travelers Rest

Travelers Rest is a small town on the rise in Greenville, SC. Located at the base of the Blue Ridge Mountains, it's a gateway to all things outdoors. I've lived in Travelers Rest for the past 3 years, but have roots in this town extending all the way back to the 1800s

I used Illustrator to design this graphical depiction of the town with Paris Mountain in the background. Animating a few frames brings it to life.
